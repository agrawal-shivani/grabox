CODINGO

Round 1 :

The participants will have to answer simple C questions and get the clues for next question.Last question will be the key to second round.

Round 2 :

Second Round will be the final round in which the participants will have to construct the code for the provided problem statement.

'C' over Snakes

Rounds :

Participants will be in the goruo of two. One member will roll the dice and another member will take the positions on the Snakes and Ladders Board. If he correctly answers the given question, he will proceed in the game.At atime, four to five teams will participate. The team which reaches the final block first or after a certain time limit, is closest to the final block will be the winner.
